Explain what the ".map" properties of arrays does.

A: .map, acts like it is a "for" or "forEach" loop, looping through an array and returning every single element in that array. 

Explain what the ".reduce" properties of arrays does.

A: .reduce, acts almost as the same as .map however you can choose which elements you want it to return back to you.

How is ".map" different from reduce?

A: You can manipulate the Data to get the information you need or modify it. However you can't change the amount of elements you get from it.

What are "switch" statements?

A: Switch statements acts like a "if/else" statement, however it evaluates an expression checking that expression value to a case and executes whatever statements are associated with that case
